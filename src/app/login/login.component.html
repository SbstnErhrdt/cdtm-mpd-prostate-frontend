Welcome to ProCare App

The ProCare ecosystem allows prostate cancer patients to track their symptoms, medication and measurements with an easy-to-use interface. It also supports external devices to help with the interaction. If you are first time user then please login using the credentials provided to you by your doctor. If you want to become a user, please click on the info page or talk to your doctor about it.
